Rates:

Overall, rafting is family and kid-friendly, but pay attention to general regulations and guides on difficulty. Since prices vary depending on the type of rafting trip, location, and length of trip, this page will dive into the different offers and take a look at the various options in our rivers that are famous for whitewater rafting.

Classic Whitewater Rafting

Our classic river whitewater rafting trip is perfect for those looking to raft in a larger group and wanting to hit some fun rapids such as the Lunch Counter and The Big Kahuna!. The Classic Whitewater rafting trip is 3.5 hours round trip, and there is room for 8-10 paddlers as well as room in the middle of the boat if you don’t want to paddle.

Adults $40

Children $20

Book Now

Small Boat Whitewater

This is the River rafting trip for you if you’re feeling adventurous! Our Small Boat Slam boasts the smallest whitewater rafting commercial boats on the river. The smaller the boat, the bigger the hit! This rafting trip is 3.5 hours long, and our raft has enough room for 6 paddlers and a couple of bull riders. All safety gear is provided, as well as round-trip transportation. The minimum age for the Small Boat Slam is 6 years old.

Adults $50

Children $30

Book Now

Scenic Float

Our River float trip allows you to relax and let our experienced guide do the work for you. This 13-mile trip on the River follows along the south border of river which is known for its immense beauty and being the best bald eagle habitat in the Lower 48. You might also see some moose, deer, elk, coyotes, beavers, or marmots on the trip, as well as an abundance of avian wildlife, such as pelicans and osprey.

Adults $30

Children $10
